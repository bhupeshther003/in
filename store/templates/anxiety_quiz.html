Anxiety Quiz

1. Over the past two weeks, how often have you been bothered by feeling nervous, anxious, or on edge?

2. Have you been unable to stop or control worrying over the past two weeks?

3. How often have you experienced restlessness, such as fidgeting or being unable to sit still?

4. Have you become easily fatigued or felt tired often in the past two weeks?

5. Have you had difficulty concentrating or your mind going blank over the past two weeks?
